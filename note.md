# Customer Shopping Data Analysis

## Methodology

### 1. Data Loading

The dataset was loaded into Pandas for analysis.

### 2. Data Quality Check

* Checked dataset dimensions.
* Inspected column data types.
* Checked missing values.
* Checked duplicate records.

### 3. Data Preparation

* Converted `invoice_date` to datetime format.
* Extracted Year and Month from the invoice date.

### 4. Data Analysis

The following analyses were performed:

* Spending by Category
* Spending by Shopping Mall
* Spending by Payment Method
* Spending by Gender
* Average Purchase Amount per Category
* Revenue Ranking by Category
* Shopping Mall vs Category Pivot Table
* Yearly Revenue Trend (2021–2023)
* Male vs Female Spending Comparison

### 5. Visualizations

Created the following visualizations:

* Revenue Heatmap (Shopping Mall vs Category)
* Yearly Revenue Trend by Category
* Gender vs Spending by Category (Grouped Bar Chart)

---

## Business Insights

1. A small number of product categories generate the majority of total revenue.

2. Shopping malls differ in revenue performance, suggesting location-based customer preferences.

3. Customer payment behavior highlights the most preferred payment methods for purchases.

4. Male and female customers spend differently across product categories, creating opportunities for targeted promotions.

5. Yearly revenue trends help identify growing categories and support long-term inventory planning.
